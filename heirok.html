    About Heirok Village

    History & Geography

    Heirok is a prominent town and Nagar Panchayat located in the Thoubal district of Manipur, India. Situated near the Wangjing River, it is known for its fertile lands and rich agricultural heritage.

    Quick Facts

    • District: Thoubal
    • State: Manipur [IN-MN]
    • Language: Meiteilon
